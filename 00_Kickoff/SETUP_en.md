# Clear your backlog -- Course Overview

Welcome to the seminar **Clear your backlog** (Kunsthochschule Kassel, New Media, Summer Semester 2026).

This course works through the "backlog" that most artists and designers keep pushing ahead of themselves: website, CV, statement, portfolio, applications. By the end of the semester, you will have a functional **professional toolkit** for your artistic practice.

---

## Format

- **Rhythm:** Every two weeks, approx. 7 sessions across the semester
- **Duration:** 6 hours per session -- 2 hrs input -- break -- 4 hrs exercises and worktime
- **Language:** German/English (materials bilingual DE/EN)
- **Setup:** Your own laptop with [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) installed -- for every session

---

## What you will have in hand at the end

- Structured Obsidian vault as a working base
- Work descriptions for your central pieces
- Artist/designer statement (DE + EN)
- Professional CV
- Work documentation (photo, video, screenshots)
- Tech rider for at least one work
- Online website with your content
- Portfolio PDF for applications
- Overview of the funding landscape (grants, residencies, festivals)
- Your own "future plan" for the next professional steps

## Way of Working

- **Hands-on:** Every session produces something that goes directly into your vault.
- **Iterative:** Texts and materials are revised, not delivered once.
- **Peer feedback:** Pair work and group rounds are part of every session.
- **Own material:** Bring your own work -- everything is built around your projects.
