# Short Bio

[Your Name] is a [medium/discipline]-based artist living and working in [City]. Their work explores [main theme/approach] through [media/techniques]. [One sentence about current focus, recent exhibition, or notable achievement.]

---

## Tips for Writing Your Short Bio

- **Length:** 2-4 sentences (max. 500 characters)
- **Person:** Write in third person ("She explores..." / "They work with...")
- **Content:** Name, discipline, location, core theme, current relevance
- **Tone:** Professional but accessible
- **Use cases:** Exhibition labels, festival programs, social media profiles, press releases

## Variants

You may need different versions:

- **Ultra-short (1 sentence):** For Instagram bios, conference badges
- **Standard (2-4 sentences):** For exhibition materials, website "About" section
- **Extended (1 paragraph):** For catalog texts, press kits

---

## Template

**Ultra-short:**
[Name] is a [discipline] artist based in [City], working with [medium].

**Standard:**
[Name] is a [discipline] artist based in [City]. Their practice centers on [theme], using [media/techniques] to [what the work does/explores]. [Recent context: exhibition, award, project.]

**Extended:**
[Name] (*[birth year], [birth place]) is a [discipline] artist living and working in [City]. Their work investigates [theme/question] through [media and methods]. Drawing on [influences/references], [Name] creates [type of work] that [effect on viewer/purpose]. Recent exhibitions include [exhibition] at [venue] ([year]) and [exhibition] at [venue] ([year]). [Name] holds a [degree] from [institution].
