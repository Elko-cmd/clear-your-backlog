# Artist Statement

[Write your statement here. Use the structure below as a guide.]

---

## Structure Guide

**Entry -- What do I do?**
Name the media, formats, and focal points of your practice. Be specific.

**Deepening -- Why?**
What questions drive your work? What interests you thematically and formally?

**Method -- How do I work?**
What processes, tools, or strategies are central to your practice?

**Impact -- What should the audience experience?**
What should viewers/users understand, feel, or take away?

---

## Tips

- Write in first person, active voice, present tense
- Name specific media (not just "mixed media" -- say what you actually use)
- Avoid buzzwords ("innovative," "unique," "pushing boundaries") -- be concrete and authentic
- Show what makes your approach distinctive
- Connect technical and conceptual aspects
- Length: 100-300 words for most contexts

## After Writing

- Let it rest for a few days
- Revise with distance
- Have others read it (including non-artists)
- Adapt for specific contexts (portfolio, application, exhibition, website)

**Note:** A statement is never "finished" -- it evolves with your practice. It can be useful to maintain different versions for different contexts.
